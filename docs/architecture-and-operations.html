Architecture and Operations

Codex emits OTLP signals to a local Collector. The Collector redacts selected attributes, batches records, and exports them to the LGTM backend.

CLI / DesktopAgent run
OTLP:4318 logs, traces, metrics

Builder

Playbooksdebug and optimize

Signals

Logs

Event-level records such as websocket events, SSE completions, warnings, startup phases, and token-bearing completion records.

Traces

Execution spans that reveal latency inside Codex startup, session handling, model request flow, and tool/MCP operations.

Metrics

Collector health and Tempo-generated spanmetrics such as call rate and p95 latency.

Privacy

Codex is configured with log_user_prompt = false. The collector also drops identity and prompt-like attributes before storage.

delete_key(attributes, "user_email")
delete_key(attributes, "user_account_id")
delete_key(attributes, "prompt")
delete_key(attributes, "user_prompt")
